    What is a myocardial infarction ?

    A myocardial infarction, also known as a heart attack, is a medical emergency in which the heart muscle begins to die due to a lack of blood flow. This is usually caused by a blockage in the arteries that supply blood to the heart. If a healthcare provider does not restore blood flow quickly, irreversible damage and death are the main consequences.
